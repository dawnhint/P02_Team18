Basic Branding Ideas

1/What does the company do, or offer?

Leafina is a tea company that offers a curated selection of high-quality teas sourced from around the world.

2/Who is the company to potential customers?

Tea lovers, connoisseurs, and those seeking a chic tea experience are some of Leafina's potential customers.

3/Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

Leafina entices customers with its exceptional quality teas, promising a delightful tea experience and providing a carefully curated selection that encourages exploration and discovery.
