About Leafina

Leafina is an organic tea company that grows, processes, wholesales and retails tea. with 26 tea gardens and 15 processing centers in 12 countries around the world, and brick and mortar stores and sales websites in 67 countries, Leafina produces and sells the best organic tea with the goal of making the best and most expensive tea available to people around the world.

Benefits of Tea

The benefits of drinking tea are known to be obvious. Drinking tea can reduce the risk of disease and can keep a person healthy and living a long life. Drinking tea can increase the various trace elements, vitamins and minerals that the body needs. Experiments have shown that people who drink tea every day live two to three times longer than those who do not drink tea. At the same time, drinking tea promotes mental health and makes it easier to be a successful person.
